Enjoy Nature in luxury

Pacific Trails Resort offers a special lodging experience on the California North Coast with panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean. Your stay at the Pacific Trail Resort includes a sumptuously appointed private yurt and a cooked-to-order breakfast each morning.

  • Relax in our lodge while enjoying complimentary food and beverages
  • Savor nightly fine dining with ocean view
  • Unwind in the heated outdoor pool and whirlpool
  • Enjoy the coast on your own or join our guided tours
